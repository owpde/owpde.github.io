One World PDE Seminar


Hosted by the University of Bath and Heriot-Watt University

With the mass cancellation of in-person conferences and seminars due to the coronavirus pandemic, research communities need novel ways to stay connected. The One World PDE Seminar aims to provide such a venue for the PDE community, accessible to as many researchers as possible.

Please join us in this exciting experiment!

Mailing list

There is a mailing list which announces the speakers for each week. To subscribe, send a blank email with the subject subscribe owpde YOUR NAME HERE to sympa@lists.bath.ac.uk. You can unsubscribe in a similar way as explained in the initial confirmation email when you join.
