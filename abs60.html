Optimal mixing in incompressible flows

I will discuss transport of passive scalars by incompressible flows (such as ink in water) and measures of optimal mixing and stirring. In particular, I will present recent results concerning examples of flows that achieve the optimal mixing rate, when the flow satisfies certain physical constraints, such as an energy or a power budget, expressed as bounds on Sobolev norms of the velocity field.
