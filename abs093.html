Massimiliano Morini

University of Parma

Long-time behavior of discrete volume-preserving mean curvature flows

We consider the Almgren–Taylor–Wang implicit time-discretization scheme for the volume-preserving mean curvature flow in \(N\)-dimensions and study its long-time behavior for arbitrary bounded initial sets of finite perimeter. Open problems and possible generalizations will be discussed.
