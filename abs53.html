Wilhelm Schlag

Semiclassical spectral theory and nonlinear waves

I will discuss my recent work with Joachim Krieger (EPFL) and Shuang Miao (Wuhan) on rigidity of blowup solutions to critical wave maps under nonequivariant perturbations.
