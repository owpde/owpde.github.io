Nader Masmoudi

Courant Institute & NYU Abu-Dhabi

Recent advances in nonlinear inviscid damping

We will review some recent results dealing with inviscid damping. These results include the optimality of the Gevrey regularity required to get the damping as well as the generalization to more general monotone flows.
