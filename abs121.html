In-Jee Jeong

Seoul National University

Instabilities in vortex ring dynamics

We consider vortex ring dynamics for incompressible Euler equations. We shall explain how to obtain the following infinite norm growth results: (i) filamentation (formation of a long tail) from a traveling vortex ring, and (ii) vortex stretching from the "head-on collision" of two counter-rotating vortex rings. This is based on joint works with Kyudong Choi (UNIST).
