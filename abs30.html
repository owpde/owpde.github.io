André Neves

University of Chicago

Counting minimal surfaces in negatively curved 3-manifolds

I will survey some of the recent progress regarding existence of minimal hypersurfaces and then I will talk about my joint work with Calegari and Marques where we asymptotically count minimal surfaces in negatively curved manifolds.
